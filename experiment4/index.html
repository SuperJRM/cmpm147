Experiment 4A - Infinite Worlds

  • Experiment 4B

Description

Generates an infinitely explorable world of unique islands and a constantly changing ocean. Search for treasure by clicking on tiles, and maybe you'll find a gold coin if you're lucky!

Technical Description

We used the XXH32 hashing function, as well as p5.js's built-in noiseSeed and randomSeed methods, to generate the placement of various types of tiles over a grid. Depending on the values generated, the tiles will either take the form of a terrain tile or a shade of ocean, with the potential of for a wave forming. Clicking the land will alter its appearance and have a cance to spawn a gold coin. By placing these features within an infinitely generated landscape, not only will each user's journey be different depending on where they go, but also the seed their world is built from, making for a truly infinite world.

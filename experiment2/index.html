Experiment 2 - Living Impressions

Description

Channel the power of the stars by using your cursor to shift the night sky!

Technical

Randomly placed stars are created and added to a list, with the canvas rotating each frame, allowing them to be printed at different angles, simulating movement. As the mouse moves, the speed of the rotation accelerates by adding an acceleration value to the rotation, allowing the stars to speed across the night sky and slow down over time. Trees are stored in a list structure similar to the stars, however, they are drawn seperately, allowing them to keep their location on the canvas.

Reflection

The lows, however, definitely came from my busy schedule preventing me from doing as much work as I had originally wanted. Despite this, I had a lot of fun with this project!
